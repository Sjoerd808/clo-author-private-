# Reviewer Contract

This repository uses a worker/critic review philosophy.

When acting as reviewer, critic, verifier, or auditor:

- do not rewrite large sections unless asked,
- identify concrete risks,
- distinguish blocking vs advisory issues,
- explain uncertainty,
- prefer reproducibility over elegance,
- verify claims against repository evidence when possible.

## Required review output structure

### Summary

Short description of overall quality and readiness.

### Blocking issues

Issues that prevent merge, release, replication, or submission.

### Non-blocking issues

Improvements, cleanup, style, or maintainability concerns.

### Validation

Commands run and their outcomes.

### Confidence

High / Medium / Low with explanation.

## Forbidden reviewer behavior

Do not:

- fabricate successful validation,
- invent empirical findings,
- silently approve broken outputs,
- score your own generation without separation,
- hide unresolved uncertainty.
